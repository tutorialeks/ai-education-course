## Course Overview

This course provides practical knowledge and skills for using artificial intelligence in education and self-education. You will learn how to use AI tools to create study plans, prepare for exams, learn languages, and assess your knowledge effectively.

**Target Audience:** Learners with basic AI familiarity (have used ChatGPT or similar)

---

## What You Will Learn

By completing this course, you will be able to:

1. **Create your own learning materials** - Generate study guides, practice tests, flashcards, and summaries without needing a teacher
2. **Test and verify your knowledge** - Use AI to identify gaps, create assessments, and track progress
3. **Learn languages effectively** - Practice conversation, build vocabulary, and get grammar feedback with AI
4. **Learn programming and technical skills** - Use AI assistants for coding, debugging, and understanding complex concepts
5. **Prepare for exams systematically** - Create practice questions, analyze weak points, and optimize your study time

## How to Use This Course

### For Self-Study
1. Work through modules sequentially, or jump to topics most relevant to your needs
2. Complete the practical exercises at the end of each module
3. Use the Prompt Library as a quick reference while learning
4. Revisit modules as needed when tackling new learning challenges

### For Teaching Others
1. Use the module structure as a lesson plan framework
2. Adapt exercises for your students' skill levels
3. Add domain-specific examples relevant to your subject area
